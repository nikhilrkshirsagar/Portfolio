PCHP Analytics Using AI

HIGH LEVEL APPROACH

Problem Statement

The government had funded specialized hospitals for HIV transmission prevention. According to reports from Primary Care HIV Prevention (PCHP) grantees, the allocated funds were not fully utilized. The Health Resources and Services Administration (HRSA) expressed willingness to support the grantees.

Solution

Received detailed reports from all grantees outlining the barriers to fund utilization. Employed the AI's NER model to categorize these barriers and delivered the analytics to HRSA.

Key Achievements

  • Minimized the manual effort required to review thousands of quarterly reports
  • Simplified the analysis of extensive descriptive text fields
  • Delivered a visually rich experience for senior client management
  • Automated the entire end-to-end process

Modern Data Analytics Platform (MDAP)

Problem Statement

Government agencies had onboarded new Federal Analysts tasked with extracting insights from terabytes of datasets. Analysts faced challenges in effectively filtering and refining datasets to develop analytical models and visualizations. The lack of streamlined processes hindered timely delivery of actionable insights.

Solution

Delivered a streamlined process focused on robust filtering mechanisms to efficiently extract relevant data subsets. Implemented advanced filtering algorithms and data categorization to enhance analysts' ability to derive meaningful insights.

Key Achievements

  • 60% reduction in analysts' time spent on developing new analytical models
  • Decreased data downtime from 6 hours to approximately 1.5 hours
  • Achieved 75% reduction in data size
  • Successfully shifted client mindset to tailored data utilization

Program Oversight Module (POM)

Problem Statement

Data essential for decision-making was distributed across various data stores, leading to inefficiencies. Accessing data elements required multiple approvals and cumbersome UI/UX page switches, hampering the productivity of federal officers making critical decisions.

Solution

Delivered a centralized Program Oversight Module within HRSA's Electronics Handbook. This unified platform enables seamless visualization and access to core information across business units, mitigating restrictions from disparate data stores.

Key Achievements

  • Centralized data without compromising governance principles
  • Delivered a robust process to avoid security breaches
  • Increased productivity by 90%
  • Reduced data availability time from 8 days to immediate access
